Academics


Alfaisal University offers wide range of Graduate and Undergraduate programs in its four colleges
(Business, Engineering, Medicine and Science).

The programs have been designed in collaboration with distinguished international academic partners in order to satisfy the local and international market demands and to enable students to be prepared for employment.

Located in the heart of Riyadh, the center for commerce, industry and research, Alfaisal offers its students opportunities to develop their full potential with outstanding facilities, inspirational academics and research-led teaching that will enable our students to leave us as highly skilled, well-developed individuals able to make the transition into the workplace easily.

Latest News


The BAE Systems Program Signing


On Thursday, the 9th of February, Alfaisal University held a special signing with BAE Systems, creating an even stronger partnership with the trusted co-founders.

Attending the signing was Abdulaziz Al-Faraj, Deputy VP Strategy & Business Development, as well as university representatives and other company delegates.


Nebras Drug awareness campaign


On Sunday the 1st of January 2017, Alfaisal University started off the new year with the opening ceremony for the National Committee for Combating Drugs (NEBRAS). The opening ceremony was sponsored by University President Dr. Mohammed Alhayaza and the guest of honor was the Secretary General of the National Committee for Combating Drugs, Mr. Abdulelah Alsharif.
